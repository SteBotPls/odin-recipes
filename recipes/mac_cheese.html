Mac and Cheese!



Mac and cheese is really good!

    Ingredients
  • Macaroni
  • Butter
  • All purpose flour
  • Salt
  • Ground pepper
  • Milk
  • Cheddar cheese
    Steps
  1. Boil water and cook macaroni while salting
  2. melt better while adding flour, salt, and pepper
  3. Gradually add milk
  4. Add cheese!
